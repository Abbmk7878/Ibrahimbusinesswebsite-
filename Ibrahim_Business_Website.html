About Us

Workshop (Ibrahim Abba Muhammad) Shopping Complex is a registered small enterprise in Yobe State, Nigeria. We aim to empower our local community by providing essential goods and services through a well-structured retail and artisan-friendly environment. We support youth and small traders by creating a safe and accessible place to grow their businesses and skills.

Our Mission

To build economic resilience in the community by offering sustainable business spaces, promoting entrepreneurship, and preserving local livelihoods in the face of environmental and economic challenges.
